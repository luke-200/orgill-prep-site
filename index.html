Pricing Review and Evaluation Program (PREP)

The Pricing Review and Evaluation Program (PREP) provides bi-weekly cost and retail updates based on a retailer’s purchase history, ensuring pricing remains current, competitive, and aligned with Orgill programs.

PREP Options

PREP Option 1 – My Store Will Print the Updated Labels ($50/month)

Retailers receive bi-weekly PREP updates with cost and retail changes based on 24 months of purchase history. Reports and tools are available through Orgill.com. Stores print their own updated labels in-house, and electronic files may still be transmitted to the POS system.

PREP Option 2 – Orgill Prints and Ships Updated Labels ($70/month)

Includes all features of Option 1, plus Orgill prints updated labels and ships them directly to the retailer. Stores may choose whether stickers are received with each Orgill truck delivery or only with the bi-weekly PREP updates.
